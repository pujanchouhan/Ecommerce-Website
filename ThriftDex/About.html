About Us - Thriftdex India We aim at providing the Indian GenZ with the coolest clothing at affordable prices without compromising on quality! Originated in 2024 by Pujan Chouhan, Thriftdex India provides the urban GenZ with their style necessities. What started as a small venture to offer stylish merchandise for Pujan's Instagram followers quickly turned into a popular fashion destination. Thriftdex India brings you second-hand clothes imported from fashion-forward countries like South Korea, Japan, and Thailand. These countries are renowned for their unique and trendy street styles. Our curated collections are sourced from international university students, ensuring you get the latest in global fashion trends. Whether it’s oversized hoodies, stylish jackets, or quirky T-shirts, every piece has a story and offers you a slice of international style. We understand the fast-paced lives of GenZ and their desire to always look fashionable without burning a hole in their pockets. Our mission is to make high-quality, stylish clothing accessible to everyone. At Thriftdex India, we believe that fashion shouldn’t be an expensive affair, and everyone deserves to look and feel great in what they wear. Our street style pieces are designed to give you a fashionable edge at affordable prices. These clothes are versatile and can be worn on various occasions—be it college, a casual brunch with friends, a night out, or just hanging out at a cafe or the mall. Wherever you go, Thriftdex ensures you are always dressed in style. These are not just clothes, they are NEEDS! Environmental sustainability is at the heart of what we do. By promoting the use of second-hand clothing, we are contributing to reducing the environmental impact of the fashion industry. Thriftdex India is committed to promoting sustainable fashion, and we take pride in offering preloved clothes that still have a lot of life left in them. We believe in quality, and every piece of clothing that comes to Thriftdex India goes through a stringent quality check to ensure it meets our high standards. You can shop with confidence, knowing that you are getting well-maintained, stylish pieces that are as good as new. Our journey has been incredible, and we owe it all to our loyal customers who have trusted us with their fashion needs. Thriftdex India is more than just a store; it’s a community of fashion enthusiasts who believe in sustainable, affordable fashion. We are excited about the future and look forward to continuing to bring you the best in thrift fashion. Thriftdex India is a lifestyle products brand under parent company Auxo Entertainment. Join us on this exciting journey and let Thriftdex India be your go-to destination for all your fashion needs. We promise to keep you ahead of the fashion curve with our ever-evolving collections.
